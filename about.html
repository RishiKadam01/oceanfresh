About OceanFresh

OceanFresh was founded with a mission to deliver the freshest seafood caught daily from the Arabian Sea right to your doorstep. We pride ourselves on sourcing premium quality Surmai, Pomfret, Prawns, and more directly from trusted local fishermen who share our passion for sustainability and excellence.

Our commitment extends beyond freshness — we ensure every catch meets strict quality control standards to guarantee taste, texture, and nutrition. Whether you're a professional chef or a home cook, OceanFresh brings the sea to your plate with convenience and trust.

Our eco-friendly packaging keeps your seafood chilled and ready to cook, while our customer-first service makes ordering seamless and reliable. Experience the ocean’s bounty with OceanFresh — where quality meets freshness.
